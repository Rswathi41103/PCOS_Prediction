Welcome to PCOS.

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a prevalent hormonal disorder affecting many women.

Our app is designed to assist women with PCOS by offering top-quality information from leading experts in the field. Created with the input of women who have PCOS, our app is tailored to meet your needs.

Living with PCOS involves a continuous journey of understanding the condition, recognizing its effects on your life, and discovering how to manage it effectively. We provide reliable information to help you understand PCOS and guide you towards achieving the healthiest lifestyle possible.

Thriving - Flourishing

Living with PCOS can be particularly challenging when it comes to maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Women with PCOS are more prone to weight gain, and an unhealthy lifestyle can exacerbate health issues more significantly for them compared to those without the condition. To help you thrive and manage PCOS, we recommend the following strategies for living your healthiest life:

  • Improve diet and physical activity for physical and mental health
  • Prevent weight gain (really important goal)
  • Modest weight loss (if deemed relevant by women and clinicians)
